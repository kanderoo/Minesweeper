Minesweeper is the classic strategy game you remember. Your goal is to open all of the cells except for the mines. In each cell there is either a bomb, a number, or a blank space. The numbers will tell you how many bombs are in the surrounding 8 spaces. The blank cells will open a large chunk of cells. The bombs, of couse, blow up. To mark a cell that you think is a bomb, simply right click the cell to flag it. Upon opening all of the cells without opening any of the bombs, you win!
